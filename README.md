# ✨ Overview

The **Mobile UI Screens Challenge** is a self-driven UX/UI design project completed entirely in **Figma**.

This challenge was created to improve practical UI design skills by analyzing, recreating, and redesigning interfaces from some of the world's most widely used mobile applications.

Over the course of **30 Days**, I explored various product categories including:

- Messaging Apps
- Social Media Platforms
- Streaming Services
- Productivity Tools
- Finance Applications
- Food Delivery Apps
- Travel Platforms
- Weather Applications
- E-Commerce Products
- Creator Tools

Rather than simply copying screens, the goal was to understand:

- Why interfaces are designed the way they are
- How users interact with products
- What creates good visual hierarchy
- How consistency improves usability

   ↓

   ↓

   ↓

   ↓
Present Final Design

# 🎨 Design Principles Applied

Throughout the challenge, several design principles remained consistent.

---

## Visual Hierarchy

Users should immediately understand:

- What is important
- What is clickable
- What requires attention

---

## Consistency

Maintaining consistency across:

- Components
- Typography
- Spacing
- Navigation Patterns

---

## Accessibility

Design decisions considered:

- Readability
- Touch Targets
- Color Contrast
- Mobile Usability

---

## Simplicity

Reducing unnecessary complexity while improving usability.

---

## Scalability

Creating layouts that could easily grow into complete design systems.
